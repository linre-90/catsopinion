Our privacy and cookie policy!

updated: 12.2.2021

Cats opinion's virtual strategy

Our policy is to offer user safe experience and respect their privacy. This is why we don't collect any unnecessary data. We will be very open and informative about anything we migth collect.

-Website should be for user, user should not be data source!

What user data we store?

Cat cast does not collect or store any sensitive/personal user data! Cookie acceptance is mainly for 3rd party cookies. This is stored in web browser, and it can be deleted at any time. CatsOpinion cookies do not save any user data.

Users email addresses

CatsOpinion does not store or distribute those email addresses to anyone. When user wants to be contacted and leaves email at contact page, email address is stored only as long is necessery to communicate. After that email address will be removed from our systems permanently and never seen again.
